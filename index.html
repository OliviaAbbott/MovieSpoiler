Despicable Me

Released:2010

Starring

  • Steve Carell
  • Jason Segel
  • Miranda Cosgrove

Movie Quote

"It's so fluffy I'm gonna die!"

Plot

Gru, a super villain, who is scheming to steal the moon, adopts three orphan girls to help with his evil plot. He plans on using them to steal things from one of his enemies. However, as he gets to know the girls, his cold heart begins to thaw. While going through with his evil plans, Gru finds that he cares more about the girls than being a villain. He gives up his plan in order to save the girls allowing them to continue to be in his life.
